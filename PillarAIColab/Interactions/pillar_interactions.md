## 🔗 Core Interaction Mappings

### 1. Growth & Expansion
* **Attraction + Cohesion**: **Colony Formation**. [cite_start]Units gather and stick to high-value goals or resource nodes, creating dense organizational clusters[cite: 52, 64, 84].
* **Force + Influence**: **Rapid Scaling**. [cite_start]Direct energy application combined with wide propagation results in explosive network growth[cite: 40, 41].
* **Warmth + Cohesion**: **High-Trust Safety**. [cite_start]Units bind through mutual support and psychological safety rather than forced conformity[cite: 47, 64].
* **Attraction + Relation**: **Market Demand**. [cite_start]Directional pull creates new dependency links and resource exchange pathways[cite: 49, 65].

### 2. Stability & Defense
* **Resistance + Integrity**: **Systemic Persistence**. [cite_start]The primary defensive state that allows a unit to survive external Force or Harm without losing its identity[cite: 41, 42, 62].
* [cite_start]**Memory + Integrity**: **Stable Specialization**: Consistency is maintained by anchoring current actions to historical identity and learned expertise[cite: 42, 48].
* [cite_start]**Awareness + Resistance**: **Risk Mitigation**: Perception of reality allows for the proactive deployment of stabilizers before Harm occurs[cite: 34, 41].
* [cite_start]**Willpower + Resistance**: **Static Defense**: The ability to hold a position or state against overwhelming external pressure[cite: 38, 41].

### 3. Execution & Impact
* **Force + Awareness**: **Precision Execution**. [cite_start]Energy is applied to the exact correct target based on accurate data perception[cite: 34, 39, 40].
* **Force + Willpower**: **Sustained Impact**. [cite_start]Ensures that the application of energy remains consistent until the goal is achieved[cite: 38, 40].
* **Presence + Influence**: **Broadcast Dominance**. [cite_start]A high-visibility signal that overrides other system inputs to command attention[cite: 41, 46].
* **Relation + Force**: **Leveraged Output**. [cite_start]Applying energy through a network of dependencies to achieve results greater than the sum of parts[cite: 40, 45].

### 4. Transformation & Adaptation
* **Harm + Memory**: **Adaptive Evolution**. [cite_start]The system stores the cause of disruption to trigger future avoidance and structural refactoring[cite: 48, 50, 70].
* **Harm + Willpower**: **Crisis Persistence**. [cite_start]Maintaining a strategic trajectory despite taking active damage; leads to high-stress breakthroughs[cite: 38, 50].
* **Warmth + Harm**: **Regenerative Healing**. [cite_start]Supportive energy enables the system to repair damage and transform after a disruption cycle[cite: 47, 50].
* [cite_start]**Distortion + Harm**: **Invisible Decay**: Damage that is not detected by Awareness, leading to silent systemic failure[cite: 50, 70].

### 5. Failure Modes (Conflict & Decay)
* **Distortion + Influence**: **Propaganda**. [cite_start]The spread of internal states or information that does not align with systemic reality[cite: 41, 70].
* **Presence + Distortion**: **Masked Incompetence**. [cite_start]High visibility used to hide a lack of data accuracy or internal consistency[cite: 46, 70].
* **Warmth + Force (Low)**: **Complacency**. [cite_start]High comfort and support leading to a total lack of execution or output[cite: 40, 47].
* **Cohesion + Distortion**: **Echo Chamber**. [cite_start]A group bound so tightly that they propagate and reinforce noise, ignoring external Awareness[cite: 44, 70].
* **Relation + Harm**: **Cascading Failure**. [cite_start]Damage in one central unit propagates through its network connections to destabilize neighbors[cite: 45, 50].

### 6. Throughput & Resilience
* **Flux + Force**: **Momentum**. [cite_start]High transfer bandwidth allows force to propagate across multiple timesteps, increasing effective execution and reducing bottlenecks[cite: 15, 16].
* **Depth + Integrity**: **Durability**. [cite_start]Latent capacity provides internal volume that prevents Integrity from cracking under high Resistance or disruptive stress[cite: 15, 42].
* **Flux + Memory**: **Recollection Speed**. [cite_start]Throughput determines how quickly history and past context can be pulled into the active working window[cite: 10, 15].
* **Depth + Willpower**: **Ambition**. [cite_start]Stored capacity defines the scale of multi-step planning and the size of achievable goal states[cite: 15, 38].
* **Flux + Depth**: **Stability Check**. [cite_start]When Flux exceeds Depth, the system enters turbulence; when Depth far exceeds Flux, the system becomes ossified and unable to express its latent potential[cite: 15, 16].
